About Us

Your Trusted Stay in Kathmandu

Kailash Hotel offers a peaceful sanctuary for pilgrims and travelers visiting the holy Pashupatinath Temple. Located just 5 minutes walk from the temple, our hotel provides clean, comfortable accommodations with a touch of Nepali hospitality.

We understand the needs of Indian pilgrims and ensure vegetarian food options, prayer facilities, and a serene environment for your spiritual journey.
